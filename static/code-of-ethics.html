Code of Ethics

TL;DR

I'll be nice ;)

Respect

I will have care, concern, fairness, and civility toward others while recognizing and embracing each individual's dignity, freedom, and diversity even in the face of disagreement.

Integrity

I will act and communicate truthfully and candidly. I will conduct myself free of personal conflicts or appearances of impropriety and self-dealing.

Communication

I will openly share information with you as possible as I can.

Excellence

I will strive to meet the high expectations I have set for myself as well as the expectations of you.

Responsibility

I will be trustworthy and answerable for my conduct, decisions and obligations and will comply with all applicable laws, regulations, policies and procedures.
I recognize my obligation to report unethical conduct to appropriate authorities.
